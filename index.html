About Genvision


Genvision - the signature annual event of Symbiotek Council, is aimed at providing a platform for exploring innovative and creative ideas to guide you towards a great career in Biosciences & Bioengineering. Keeping up with the tradition of 27 successful years, we present to you GenVision 2025.


Bringing together high-quality researchers with cutting-edge expertise in basic and applied bio-related area, the event provides a platform for upcoming talents and empowers students with a scientific and technical temperament which provides them leadership for emerging needs of the society.
